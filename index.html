Meet Our Founder

The visionary behind Heal2Rise

Muhammad Waqas
Founder & CEO

Muhammad Waqas

Founder & CEO, Heal2Rise

"I worked so hard to earn my reward, but before I could even hold it in my hands, my hands were cut off. This experience taught me the importance of mental resilience and the need for support systems in our society."

Muhammad Waqas is a passionate advocate for mental health awareness and social welfare. With a vision to create a platform that bridges the gap between those in need and organizations that can help, he founded Heal2Rise to provide confidential support to individuals facing depression, family issues, and hopelessness.

Through HopeAid and Heal2Rise , Muhammad aims to transform lives by connecting people with compassionate NGOs, professional counselors, and skill development opportunities.
